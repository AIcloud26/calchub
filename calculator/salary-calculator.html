Salary Calculator

Convert your annual salary to hourly, weekly, bi-weekly, and monthly rates. Includes estimated after-tax breakdown.

Understanding Salary Conversions

Here's how we calculate your salary across different pay periods:

  • Hourly: Annual salary ÷ 2,080 hours (40 hrs × 52 weeks)
  • Weekly: Annual salary ÷ 52 weeks
  • Bi-weekly: Annual salary ÷ 26 pay periods
  • Monthly: Annual salary ÷ 12 months

How to Use This Calculator

  1. Enter your gross annual salary
  2. Select your state for more accurate tax estimates
  3. Click "Calculate Salary" to see your breakdown
  4. View your net (after-tax) income across different pay periods

⚠️ Disclaimer

Tax calculations are estimates using simplified 2024 tax brackets. For accurate tax advice, consult a tax professional.
