Understanding Compound Interest

Compound interest is often called the "eighth wonder of the world" because it allows your money to grow exponentially over time. Unlike simple interest, compound interest is earned on both your initial investment and accumulated interest.

The Power of Compound Interest

If you invest $10,000 at 7% annual return for 30 years:

Future Value = $10,000 × (1 + 0.07/12)^(12×30) = ~$76,123

That's 7.6x your original investment!

How to Use This Calculator

  1. Enter your initial investment amount (one-time deposit)
  2. Add your planned monthly contribution
  3. Enter your expected annual return rate (historical S&P 500 average is ~7%)
  4. Select your investment time horizon in years
  5. Click "Calculate Investment" to see your projected returns
