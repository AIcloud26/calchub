Retirement Calculator

Plan for your retirement by estimating your future retirement fund and monthly retirement income.

The 4% Rule

Our calculator uses the "4% Rule" to estimate your monthly retirement income. This rule suggests that you can safely withdraw 4% of your retirement savings annually without running out of money for at least 30 years.

Monthly Income = (Retirement Fund × 0.04) / 12

How to Use This Calculator

  1. Enter your current age and planned retirement age
  2. Input your current retirement savings (401k, IRA, etc.)
  3. Add how much you contribute monthly
  4. Enter your expected annual return rate
  5. Click "Calculate Retirement" to see your projections

💡 Tip

Start saving as early as possible. The power of compound interest means that money you save in your 20s has decades to grow.

Frequently Asked Questions

The 4% rule is a guideline, not a guarantee. Some financial experts suggest being more conservative (3-3.5%) given current market conditions, while others argue 4% is still reasonable for a 30-year retirement.
