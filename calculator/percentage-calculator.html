Percentage Calculator

Calculate percentages in three different modes. Find what percent a number is, calculate percentage of a number, or find percent change.

Percentage Formulas

What is X% of Y?

Result = (X / 100) × Y

X is what percent of Y?

Result = (X / Y) × 100

Percent Change

Result = ((New - Old) / Old) × 100

How to Use

  1. Select your calculation mode using the toggle buttons
  2. Enter the required values in the input fields
  3. Click "Calculate" to see your result
  4. The formula and explanation will be displayed below

Frequently Asked Questions

Use the "% Change" mode. Enter the original value as "Old Value" and the new value as "New Value". A positive result indicates an increase, while a negative result indicates a decrease.

"Percent" is the mathematical concept (represented by the symbol %), while "percentage" is the result you get when you multiply a number by a percent. For example, 50% is a percent, and 25 is the percentage when you calculate 50% of 50.
