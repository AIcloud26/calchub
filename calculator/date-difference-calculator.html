Date Calculator

Calculate the exact difference between two dates in days, weeks, months, and years. Perfect for counting down to events or calculating durations.

Common Uses for Date Calculators

  • Pregnancy countdown: Calculate how far along you are or your due date
  • Project duration: Determine how long a project will take
  • Contract periods: Calculate length of employment or subscriptions
  • Age verification: Check exact age for legal purposes
  • Event planning: Count down to weddings, vacations, or holidays

How to Use This Calculator

  1. Select your start date using the date picker
  2. Select your end date
  3. Click "Calculate Difference"
  4. View the complete breakdown including days, weeks, months, and years
  5. See working days count and day of week information

Frequently Asked Questions

The calculator shows the difference between the two dates (not including the end date in the count). For example, from Jan 1 to Jan 2 is 1 day.

Our calculator shows exact years, months, and days. The "approximate months" shown uses a 30.44-day average, which is useful for quick estimates but may differ slightly from the exact calculation.

Working days (also called business days) exclude Saturdays and Sundays. Holidays are not subtracted as they vary by country and year.
