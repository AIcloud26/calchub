How Age Calculation Works

Our age calculator determines your exact age by calculating the difference between your birth date and today's date. It accounts for leap years and varying month lengths to give you precise results.

How to Use

  1. Select your date of birth using the date picker above
  2. Click "Calculate Age" to see your results
  3. View your exact age breakdown
  4. See how many days until your next birthday
